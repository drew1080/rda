Architecture Tours
Since 1975, tours have helped RDA members experience the most interesting works of architecture, landscape, and interior design in the city.

Lectures
Fall and spring lecture series bring architects, designers, engineers, writers, and artists from around the world to engage and educate the community.

Galas
Annual galas allow us to come together to recognize and celebrate those individuals and institutions making an impact on Houston’s built environment.
