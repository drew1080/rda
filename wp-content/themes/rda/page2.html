Architecture Tours

Programs

For over 30 years RDA has provided Houston with a forum for thinking about and discussing design and the built environment. RDA has brought leading designers, critics, theorists, and planners to Houston through its educational programs and the publication of Cite: The Architecture and Design Review of Houston. Each year, RDA sponsors two lecture series, an architecture tour, and two or more civic forums. Additional programs, such as exhibitions, film series, competitions, special lectures, and tours are planned to complement the existing annual schedule. RDA has sponsored over 300 lectures, 60 panel discussions and symposiums, 30 architecture tours, 20 exhibitions, nine film series, four grants awards competitions, and three national design competitions.

Lectures

RDA lecture programs have given the local community their first opportunity to see architects like Frank Gehry, Cesar Pelli, Helmut Jahn, Steven Holl, and Rem Koolhaus before they became international superstars, and Richard Meier, Aldo Rossi, Robert Venturi, Rafael Moneo, Renzo Piano, and Glenn Murcutt before they were honored with the Pritzker Prize.
